Turkey And The Wolf Sandwich

Why you'll want to make this Sandwich

This is one of the best, if not the best sandwich I have ever tasted. Actually, I have never been to NOLA and never had the chance to taste the original creation. But this recipe alone, that I've just made up from the picture shown above, is a gift from god. The chips make it crunchy, the cheese makes it creamy and holds the mortadella in place (well sort of), the mortadella itself gives this sandwich flavour you haven't tasted anywhere else, the shredded lettuce (shredduce or shrettuce) gives it well deserved freshness and the combination of mustard and mayonnaise compliment each other better than any other sauce. If that description alone doesn't make you hungry, than I don't know what will.
