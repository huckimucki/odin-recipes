Reibekuchen

Why you'll want to make Reibekuchen

There just isn't anything better than coming home from a walk on a rainy afternoon and warming yourself up with a couple of Reibekuchen. The english translation potato pancake sounds like somethings children would cook, if they were allowed to mix anything together. So I'm sticking with Reibekuchen throughout this recipe.

The Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1.5kg potatoes
  • 3 onions
  • ~200g 405 wheat flour
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil
